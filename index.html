FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know before your first visit — and beyond.

What insurance plans do you accept?
+

We accept all major insurance providers including Star Health, HDFC ERGO, Niva Bupa, Aditya Birla Health, and government schemes such as Ayushman Bharat. We also offer cashless treatment for empanelled insurers. Please call ahead to confirm your specific plan is accepted.

How do I prepare for my first visit?
+

Please bring a valid government-issued photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), a list of any current medications, and relevant previous medical records or test results. Arrive 10 minutes early to complete your registration. We'll take care of the rest.

What are your clinic hours and do you have emergency services?
+

Our OPD is open Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM, and Sunday 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM for urgent consultations. For emergencies, our on-call physician line operates 24/7. Please call our emergency number +91 11 4567 0000 or proceed to the nearest A&E department.

Can I get a second opinion from one of your specialists?
+

Absolutely. We encourage patients to seek second opinions and actively welcome referrals. Simply book a specialist consultation and share any existing reports, scans, or treatment plans. Our doctors will provide an independent, thorough assessment.

Is my medical information kept private?
+

Patient confidentiality is paramount at MediSphere. All medical records are stored on encrypted, HIPAA-compliant systems with strict access controls. Your information is never shared with third parties without your explicit written consent, except as required by law.

Do you offer teleconsultation / online appointments?
+

Yes, we offer secure video consultations for follow-up visits, prescription renewals, and general medical advice. Teleconsultations are available during standard clinic hours and can be booked through our website or by calling reception.
